Quick answers

How quickly will I see changes

Many people notice calmer nights in 2–3 weeks as the schedule stabilizes and wind‑down becomes consistent.

Do I need sleep medication

CBT‑I is first‑line care and can be used with or without medication. Your therapist will guide best practice.

Can I change therapist

Yes, you can switch with one click if it doesn’t feel like a fit.
