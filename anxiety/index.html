Panic attacks explained and what to do

A panic attack feels like danger. Your heart races your chest tightens your head spins and thoughts rush in. It feels scary and it is also a surge that the body can ride out. It peaks then it settles. With the right steps you can move through it with far less fear.

In the moment plan
Ground name five things you see four you can touch three you can hear two you can smell and one you can taste.
Breathe inhale for four hold for two and exhale for six for one minute.
Reframe tell yourself this is a body surge I am safe it will pass and I can ride the wave.
Practice this with a therapist
